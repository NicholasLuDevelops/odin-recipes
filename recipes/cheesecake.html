Cheesecake

My cousin's cheesecake recipe. Absolutely the best cheesecake I've ever had. It is so freaking good. Also not very hard to make. Other than the water bath part. Just gotta make sure that it doesn't leak. Even if it does it's still a banger recipe.

Source

Ingredients:

Filling:

  • 32 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 1/3 cup sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream

Crust:

  • 1 3/4 cup of crumbs (graham crackers are a really good choice.)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 7 tbsp butter
