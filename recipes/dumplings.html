Homemade Dumplings

Homemade dumplings that my parents make. I wrote this recipe down a while back in like 2020. Anyways it's relatively simple to make unless you're one of those people who can burn water but then that's a whole nother problem lol... Although the wrapping part of the dumpling takes a bit to get use to. The recipe will make about 100 dumplings.

Source

Ingredients:

Filling:

  • 2 lbs meat (any kind ground)
  • .5 oz Ginger
  • 2-3 stalks Green Onion
  • 1 bundle(med) or 6 oz Chive
  • 1.5 lbs Chinese Cabbagge
  • 2 tablespoons Salt
  • .5 Cup Sesame Oil
  • 1 Cup Cooking Oil
  • 2 teaspoons White/Black Pepper (white pepper is better flavor imo)
  • Water from the cabbage

If adding seafood ex: Shrimp, Scallop -> Add another .5 lbs or more

Dough:

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1.25 cups Water
  • 1 teaspoon Salt

Flour:Water = 2:1 ratio
