Homemade Dumplings

Homemade dumplings that my parents make. I wrote this recipe down a while back in like 2020. Anyways it's relatively simple to make unless you're one of those people who can burn water but then that's a whole nother problem lol... Although the wrapping part of the dumpling takes a bit to get use to. The recipe will make about 100 dumplings.

Source

Ingredients:

Filling:

  • 2 lbs meat (any kind ground)
  • .5 oz Ginger
  • 2-3 stalks Green Onion
  • 1 bundle(med) or 6 oz Chive
  • 1.5 lbs Chinese Cabbagge
  • 2 tablespoons Salt
  • .5 Cup Sesame Oil
  • 1 Cup Cooking Oil
  • 2 teaspoons White/Black Pepper (white pepper is better flavor imo)
  • Water from the cabbage

If adding seafood ex: Shrimp, Scallop -> Add another .5 lbs or more

Dough:

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1.25 cups Water
  • 1 teaspoon Salt

Flour:Water = 2:1 ratio

To make the dough:

  1. Combine Flour & Salt
  2. Slowly add water while kneading dough
  3. Knead until smooth then set aside (cover the dough. We usually just use a bowl and put it upside down)

The rest of it:

Filling:

  1. Finely chop cabbage
    • Add salt and cabbage in a big bowl
    • Let sit and allow water to come out
    • squeeze cabbage to get as much water out possible
  2. Mince garlic, fine chop chive and green onion
  3. Stir meat, ginger, pepper, sesame oil, cooking oil, cabbage water together in ONE-DIRECTION
    • so pick either clockwise or counter-clockwise and stick with it.
  4. Pour veggies/seafood into the mix and then mix everything together

Dough (part 2):

  1. Make sure to coat the working surface with a generous amount of flour so the dough doesn't stick to the surface
  2. Take dough out of cover and knead until springy
  3. Take a chunk of dough and roll it into a long-ish/thin-ish log
  4. Cut log into small-ish chunks
  5. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin until thin and round. (Don't be too thin bc then it breaks easy)
  6. Place filling in center of of dough
  7. Squish the edges together. Find a video if you don't know how to do it.

Making it unraw:

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil
  2. Gently toss dumplings in
  3. Bring water to boil again
  4. Add .5 cups water to pot
  5. Bring water to boil again
  6. Repeat 2 more times
  7. When the dumplings float on the surface of the water then it's ready
